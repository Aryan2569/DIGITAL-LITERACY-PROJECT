Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

-------->>General Safety Tips

- Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone
- Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links
- Verify the identity of callers claiming to be officials
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)


-------->>UPI / Financial Safety 


- Never approve “collect requests” without verifying
- Do not scan QR codes to receive money
- Always double-check UPI IDs before sending money




-------->> Smart Usage Tips

- Install apps only from trusted sources
- Keep your device and apps updated
- Log out from shared devices
